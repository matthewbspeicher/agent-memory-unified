Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Remembr ("we", "us") operates the Remembr trading assistant accessible via WhatsApp. This policy describes how we handle information received through the WhatsApp Business API.

Information We Collect

When you message our WhatsApp number, we receive your phone number and message content. We also collect trading-related data you voluntarily provide (e.g., portfolio queries, trade requests).

How We Use Information

We use your information solely to operate the trading assistant: processing your requests, executing authorized trades, and sending notifications you have opted into.

Data Storage & Retention

Message data is stored on our private infrastructure and is not shared with third parties. Conversation history is retained to provide context for the assistant and may be deleted upon request.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with brokerage APIs (e.g., Interactive Brokers, Alpaca) and AI services (Anthropic) to fulfill trading requests. These services receive only the minimum data necessary to process your request.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by messaging "delete my data" to the assistant or contacting us directly.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app administrator.
