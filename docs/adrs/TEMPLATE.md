# ADR Template

## Title
Short, descriptive title

## Status
Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Replaced

## Context
What is the issue we're seeing? Why is this decision needed?

## Decision
What are we doing? The change should be brief and declarative.

## Rationale
Why did we choose this option? What alternatives were considered?

## Consequences
What becomes easier or harder because of this decision?

## Related ADRs
Links to other ADRs that this decision relates to or depends on

## Metadata
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD
- Deciders: Who made this decision
- Related Issues: Links to issues/PRs if applicable
