Layout Summary

Row 1 — Status Bar: Five KPI tiles for instant health check. Green/yellow/red indicators.

Row 2 — Primary Panels: Agent Activity (live feed) and Infrastructure (service status). These are your biggest blind spots, so they get top billing.

Row 3 — Secondary Panels: Validator (compact metrics from existing Bittensor page) and Open Trades (positions at a glance).

Drawers: Clicking any panel slides in a right-side drawer with full detail — agent logs, infra metrics history, full Bittensor dashboard, trade management. You never leave the page.
